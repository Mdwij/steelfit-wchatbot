About us

Who we are

Welcome to STEELFIT ENGINEERING CO, a reputable organisation based in Ahmedabad that has been producing and supplying top-quality Dished ends, Pipe Caps, Concentric & Eccentric Tori-cone, Fabricated Expansion Bellows, Coil Bending, and Swage Connection in all types of C.S. and S.S., Alloy steel, Aluminum Material of Construction according to Clients' drawing, Specification, and Code since 1983. Our company is an ISO:9001,2000 Certified Company (TUV Inspection) and also IBR (Indian Boiler Regulations) Approved. We take pride in our commitment to providing our clients with top-notch products and services that meet all known specifications and standards. Our company has collaborated with reputable consultants and inspection agencies like LRA, Bureau Veritas, TUV, EIL, PDIL, Jacob H & G, DNV, SGS, UHDE INDIA LTD, TATA PROJECTS LTD, BARC, and several others over the years. We have made a name for ourselves in the industry and have established ourselves as a reputable brand both domestically and abroad. We supply products to the Indian and international markets for various industries, including power, petrochemicals, fertilisers, water treatment facilities, cement manufacturing, agricultural machinery, space manufacturing, etc. We have partnerships with several TPIs, including LRA, TUV, TUV sud, BVIPL, SGS, DNV GL, IBR, JACOB, and TATA. BARC, NPCIL, IRS, and other projects.
